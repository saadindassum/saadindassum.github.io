The melting pot's done cooking, and some savory freaking tunes have come from it. Rock in Spanglish is a combination of Saadin's love of Hispanic and Western culture. Rock influenced by merengue, latin pop, and bachata will be a sure-fire hit to the generation that grew up in Latin America in the 90s, and a delicious treat to the ears of others.
