The sound of letting yourself hope. The Happiness That Religion Needs (also the meaning of the name 'Saadin') is a rejection of cynicism, and a push to the romantics of the world to keep believing, regardless of the judgement of cynics. This release is the most ambitious in marketing and filming, which is why it is left for 2028. It concretely establishes the world of Emmamile rather than just having hints about it as background easter eggs, but it will require a sizeable budget. I don't mean Hollywood sized, but I do mean bigger than what I usually get.
