Saadin Dassum

This rock and roller has come all the way from the Andes Mountains to explore what it means to be a romantic in the world of a nihilist. Dassum sings about the anxieties of a generation, such as climate change, mental health, and modern intimacy, all while attempting to seek for and inspire hope even in the darkest of times.

Saadin received multiple accolades. Most notably, in 2020, his music video 'Glasgow Lady' was selected as a winner by the Recording Academy (yes, that very same one you're thinking of) and shown at their Grammy U Panel. In 2022, Saadin's animated music video 'Tomorrow' was an official selection of MDC's Miami Film Festival.

His name is Key. Because he plays the keys of course. And her name is Stevie because she thinks Fleetwood Mac is always absolutely rockin'. Or at least it seems like her parents did.
