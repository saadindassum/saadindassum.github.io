S06E01

 When we last heard of our favorite mad scientist, he had lost all his power. His arms, his hands, his voice... From his music to his mojo.

 Dassum climbed up a snowy peak searching for a talisman which could grant its holder a voice. Dassum was surprised by an old friend... one who sought the talisman for his own purposes. And although Saadin put up one last fight with everything he had, he was ultimately beaten to a pulp, and pushed into icy rapids. The body was never recovered.

 Two years have passed, and myths have emerged of a new booming voice which bounces off the mountains of the Andes - a voice belonging to a mysterious man. This voice belongs to a man ready to change the game and save his home. A man preparing to plunge into territory where the brave dare not go. A man working tirelessly to forge himself into what only seemed possible in those dusty recesses of your mind. These rumors may just have a hint of truth. After all, you don't get rid of me that easy.
